date - The specific date of the trading day or data point
close - The closing price of the security at the end of the trading day
YesterdayClose - The closing price from the previous trading day
YesterdayOpenLogR - Log return calculated from yesterday's opening price
YesterdayHighLogR - Log return calculated from yesterday's high price
YesterdayLowLogR - Log return calculated from yesterday's low price
YesterdayVolumeLogR - Log return calculated from yesterday's trading volume
YesterdayCloseLogR - Log return calculated from yesterday's closing price
MA10 - 10-day moving average of the closing price
MA20 - 20-day moving average of the closing price
MA30 - 30-day moving average of the closing price
DayOfWeek - Numerical representation of the day of the week (1-7)
DayOfMonth - The day number within the month (1-31)
MonthNumber - The month number within the year (1-12)
EMA10 - 10-day exponential moving average, giving more weight to recent prices
EMA30 - 30-day exponential moving average, giving more weight to recent prices
RSI - Relative Strength Index, a momentum oscillator measuring overbought/oversold conditions (0-100)
MACD - Moving Average Convergence Divergence, a trend-following momentum indicator
MACD_Signal - The signal line for MACD, typically a 9-day EMA of the MACD line
BollingerUpper - Upper band of Bollinger Bands, showing potential resistance levels
BollingerLower - Lower band of Bollinger Bands, showing potential support levels
Volatility_10 - 10-day volatility measure, indicating price fluctuation intensity
Volatility_20 - 20-day volatility measure, indicating price fluctuation intensity
Volatility_30 - 30-day volatility measure, indicating price fluctuation intensity
OBV - On-Balance Volume, combining price and volume to show buying/selling pressure
ZScore - Statistical measure showing how many standard deviations away from the mean
insider_shares - Number of shares involved in insider trading activities
insider_amount - Dollar amount of insider trading transactions
insider_buy_flag - Binary indicator (1/0) showing whether insider activity was buying
sentiment - Market sentiment score, likely derived from news or social media analysis
num_articles - Number of news articles or mentions related to the security
overnight_gap - Price difference between previous close and current open
abnormal_vol - Indicator of whether trading volume is abnormally high compared to average
volatility_5d - 5-day rolling volatility measure
volatility_20d - 20-day rolling volatility measure
momentum_5d - 5-day price momentum indicator
momentum_20d - 20-day price momentum indicator
skew_5d - 5-day price skewness, measuring asymmetry in price distribution
intraday_range - The difference between the day's high and low prices
sentiment_change - Change in sentiment score compared to previous period
